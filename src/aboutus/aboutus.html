Who We Are

We are a Canadian owned private corporation, operating coffee roasting and dry powder blending facilities in London, Ontario with distribution centres in Montreal, Québec and Vancouver, BC. Our mission statement is to be the private label coffee roaster and soluble blender/packer of choice to the North American Foodservice and Retail industries.

Our History

Founded in 1980, Heritage Coffee has grown from a regional OCS roaster to one of the Canada’s most important private label roasters. Under the guidance of one of the founders, Stuart Daw, Heritage experienced significant growth, excelling in quality, consistency and innovative packaging options for our partner customers. Now controlled by his son, Kevin Daw – Heritage excels in these values and has expanded its reach to the US market.

Certifications

We acquired many different kinds of certificate not only for food but also for fair trade. They are Canada Organic, HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points), USDA Organic, Good Manufacturing Practice, OmniDegradable, Rain Forest Alliance, and FairTrade Mark. All these certificates assure our customers how reliable "Heritage Coffee" is.

Our Customers

Our main customers are foodservice distributors, OCS operators, retail brand owners. They are located in Northern America. We started as a small size business but , now our royal customers are dedicated to helping our coffee to be distributed to other areas well. Our customers are committed to our coffee and we are trying our best to meet their demand and customize the flavours.
